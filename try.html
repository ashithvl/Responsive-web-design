ABOUT DHANALAKSHMI SRINIVASAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan College of Engineering and Technology is an emerging institute of excellence in Engineering Education located on the East Coast Road, in the Historic Town of Mamallapuram. The college is run by the Srinivasan Charitable and Educational Trust which has been in the field of education for more than a decade. The college is approved by the AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to the Asia"s largest Technical University, Anna University, Chennai and Accreditated by NACC with "A" Grade Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan College of Engineering and Technology is located in a scenic beauty surrounded on two sides by a lake and on the other side by a coconut grove, providing the right ambience for pursuing Higher Education. The college is located in a sprawling area of around 50 acres which is well connected by the East Coast Road (New Mahabalipuram Road) on one side and the IT express highway (Old Mahabalipuram Road) on the other side.
